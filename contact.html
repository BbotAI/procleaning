What Happens After You Reach Out

Here's exactly what to expect — no mystery, no runaround.

1. You Reach Out

Call, text, or fill out the form on this page. Tell us a bit about your home and what kind of cleaning you're looking for. There's no commitment at this stage.

2. We Follow Up Fast

Jennifer or a team member gets back to you quickly — usually the same day. We'll ask a few questions to make sure we understand exactly what you need.

3. You Get an Honest Quote

We give you a clear, straightforward price. No hidden fees, no surprise add-ons. Just an honest number based on your actual home and needs.

4. We Schedule Your Clean

Once you're happy with the quote, we pick a day and time that works for you. We show up when we say we will — no waiting around wondering if we're coming.

5. We Get to Work

We arrive on time, bring everything we need, and get to work. You don't have to lift a finger. We take care of everything from start to finish.

6. You Come Home to Clean

Walk in to a fresh, clean space. If anything wasn't exactly right, just let us know and we'll make it right. That's the Pro Cleaning promise, every time.
