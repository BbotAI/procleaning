Hello, I'm Jennifer

My name is Jennifer Diehl, and I own Pro Cleaning Services. I've been cleaning homes and offices for over ten years — not because I had to, but because I genuinely love the feeling of walking into a space and leaving it better than I found it.

This business grew from the relationships I built with my clients over the years. When they started referring me to their friends and family, I knew it was time to take that next step. Pro Cleaning Services is built entirely on word of mouth and the trust people have placed in me — and I don't take that lightly for a single second.

We're fully insured and bonded, which means your home is protected every time we come through the door. That's not just a legal formality — it's a commitment that I'm a professional who shows up prepared and accountable, every single time.

I started this company in 2020, and every year since then has been about earning more trust, doing better work, and making sure the people I serve feel genuinely cared for.

Why Hire Local

When you invite someone into your home to clean, you're trusting them with your personal space. I understand how big that is. Some of my favorite clients — especially ladies who've kept their homes themselves for years and years — have told me how strange it felt at first to let someone else in. I completely understand that feeling, and I take that responsibility seriously every single visit.

When you call Pro Cleaning Services, you're not calling a 1-800 number or dealing with a franchise. You get me — or someone I've personally trained and trust — showing up with a smile, doing honest work, and treating your home like it matters.

I live out here too. I know these communities, I know these families, and I genuinely care about Salina and Saline County. That's not a marketing line. It's just true.

Hiring local means your money stays here, your cleaner knows your preferences, and you can call a real person if you ever have a question or concern. That's the way it should be.
