A Thorough Clean for Every Transition

Whether you're leaving a rental, moving into a new home, or turning over a property — we leave the space spotless.

Move in and move out cleans are a different kind of job. They require going further than a regular cleaning — into cabinets and drawers, inside the refrigerator and oven, behind appliances, and into all the spaces that get overlooked during routine visits.

Jennifer and her team approach every transition clean with the thoroughness it deserves. The goal is simple: leave the space in better condition than we found it, so the next person who walks through the door is starting fresh.

We work with individual tenants, homeowners, landlords, and property managers throughout Salina and Saline County. However you need us, we're flexible and straightforward to work with.

Move In / Move Out Cleaning Includes

  • All rooms — floors, walls, baseboards, window ledges
  • Kitchen — inside and outside all cabinets and drawers
  • Appliances — inside refrigerator, oven, microwave, and dishwasher
  • Bathrooms — top to bottom including grout and tile
  • Inside all closets — shelves, rods, and floors
  • Light fixtures, ceiling fans, switch plates, and vents
  • Interior windows and window sills
  • Doors and door frames throughout

Have a specific checklist from your landlord or property manager? Share it with us — we'll make sure we cover every item.

Who We Help

Move in and move out cleaning is for anyone navigating a property transition.

Tenants Moving Out

Want your security deposit back? We give rental properties a spotless clean that satisfies even the most thorough landlord inspection — and we know what they look for.

Homeowners Moving In

Even a freshly listed home can use a good clean before you move in. We make sure the space is truly clean and ready for you — your family's clean, not the previous owner's.

Landlords & Property Managers

We help you turn over rental units quickly and thoroughly. A clean property rents faster and attracts better tenants. We can work on your timeline to minimize vacancy.

Real Estate Transactions

Pre-sale and post-closing cleans that make a strong impression. We help sellers present their home at its best and help buyers move in with confidence.

How to Schedule Your Move In / Move Out Clean

Scheduling is simple. Give us a call at 785-452-0793 or fill out the quote form on our contact page. Let us know the property address, approximate square footage, and when you need it done. We'll give you a clear quote and get you on the schedule.

We recommend booking at least a few days in advance, especially around the first and last of the month when moves are most common. We do our best to accommodate last-minute requests when our schedule allows.

Move in and move out cleans take longer than standard home cleaning because we go deeper. We'll give you a realistic time estimate so you can plan around it.
