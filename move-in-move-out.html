What Is Move In / Move Out Cleaning?

A move-in or move-out clean is a thorough, one-time cleaning of an entire property — usually done either before a new tenant moves in or after a previous one has moved out. It goes well beyond a standard home cleaning and covers every inch of the space: inside cabinets, appliances, closets, window tracks, and much more.

If you're a renter, a proper move-out clean is often required to get your security deposit returned. If you're a homeowner or property manager, having a professional clean done before new occupants arrive sets the right tone and protects your property's value.

Pro Cleaning Services has done hundreds of move-in and move-out cleans across central Kansas. We know exactly what to look for and how to get a space truly clean — not just surface-clean. We work with renters, homeowners, landlords, and property management companies.

We're fully insured and bonded, which gives landlords and property managers peace of mind when we're in an empty unit. We also move fast when we need to — if you have a tight turnaround between tenants, give us a call and we'll do our best to accommodate.

Move In / Move Out Cleaning FAQ

When should I schedule my move-out cleaning?

The best time is after everything has been moved out of the space. With furniture and belongings gone, we can reach every corner, wall, and floor without working around anything. It makes the clean faster and more thorough.

Will a professional move-out clean help me get my deposit back?

It absolutely can. Many landlords require professional cleaning as a condition of returning the deposit. We know what inspectors look for and make sure nothing important is missed — inside appliances, baseboards, cabinet interiors, and more.

How far in advance do I need to book?

We recommend at least a week's notice when possible, especially around the first and last of the month when moves are most common. That said, give us a call even if your timeline is tight — we'll do our best to help.

Do you work with property managers and landlords?

Yes — and we love working with them. If you manage multiple properties, we're happy to set up a regular arrangement and become your go-to cleaning team between tenants.

Can I schedule a move-in clean before my furniture arrives?

Absolutely — and that's actually the best time. An empty space is much easier to clean thoroughly. We can get every corner and surface before your things come in, so you start fresh.
