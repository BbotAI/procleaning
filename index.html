Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions we hear most often from new clients.

Are you insured and bonded?

Yes — Pro Cleaning Services is fully insured and bonded. Your home and belongings are protected on every visit. That's not just a formality; it's a promise that you're working with a real professional.

Do I need to be home while you're cleaning?

Not at all. Many of our clients give us a key or door code. We treat every home with the same care and respect whether you're there or not. Your privacy and security always come first.

What cleaning products do you use?

We bring professional-grade products that do the job right. If you have sensitivities, allergies, or a preference for specific products, just let us know when you book. We're happy to work with what you have on hand too.

How do I get a quote?

The easiest way is to fill out the form on this page or give us a call at 785-452-0793. We'll get back to you quickly, ask a few questions about your home, and give you a clear and honest price.

What areas do you serve?

We're based in Brookville and primarily serve Salina and Saline County. We also travel to McPherson, Ottawa, Lincoln, Ellsworth, and Dickinson counties. If you're not sure, just call — we'll tell you right away.

What's the difference between regular cleaning and deep cleaning?

Regular cleaning covers the everyday essentials — vacuuming, mopping, bathrooms, kitchen surfaces, and trash. A deep clean goes further: baseboards, inside appliances, behind furniture, grout, and areas that rarely get touched. Think of it as a thorough reset.

How long does a cleaning take?

It depends on the size of your home and how much cleaning is needed. A standard home cleaning typically takes 2–4 hours. A deep clean or move-in/move-out clean will usually take longer. We'll give you a realistic estimate when we quote.

Do you bring your own supplies?

Yes — we bring everything needed to do the job right. Vacuum, mop, cleaning solutions, microfiber cloths — all of it. You don't have to provide a thing unless you have specific products you'd like us to use.
