What Is Home Cleaning?

Home cleaning is the regular, recurring service that keeps your living space fresh, healthy, and comfortable. It covers all the everyday essentials — the things that build up fast if life gets busy, which it always does. Vacuuming, mopping, bathrooms, kitchen, dusting — taken care of so you don't have to think about it.

At Pro Cleaning Services, regular home cleaning isn't something we rush through. Jennifer has been doing this for over ten years, and she brings that experience and attention to detail to every visit. She notices the things that other cleaners skip — the corners, the tops of door frames, the spots behind the toilet. They matter, so we get them.

We're fully insured and bonded, so you can trust us in your home whether you're there or not. Many of our clients give us a key and come home to a clean house. Others prefer to be home the first time and then decide. Either way works — we adapt to what's comfortable for you.

We serve homes across Salina, Brookville, and the surrounding central Kansas area. If you've been looking for a cleaner you can actually trust and rely on, we'd love to talk.

What's Included in Home Cleaning

Every regular home cleaning visit covers these areas and more.

  • Vacuuming all carpets, rugs, and upholstered surfaces
  • Mopping all hard floor surfaces
  • Dusting surfaces, shelves, ceiling fans, and light fixtures
  • Scrubbing and disinfecting all bathrooms (toilets, sinks, tubs, showers)
  • Wiping down kitchen counters, stovetop, and exterior of appliances
  • Cleaning mirrors and glass surfaces throughout the home
  • Emptying all trash cans and replacing liners
  • Straightening and tidying common areas

Need something added? Just ask. We're happy to customize your clean.

Home Cleaning FAQ

How often should I schedule home cleaning?

Most clients prefer weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly visits. We'll work with whatever schedule fits your lifestyle and budget. Some clients start monthly and move to bi-weekly once they see how much easier it makes life.

Can I get the same cleaner every time?

Yes — when possible, and we make it a priority. Consistency matters to us. When your cleaner knows your home and your preferences, the job gets done better and faster. Jennifer personally oversees this.

What if I'm not happy with the clean?

Just let us know. We'll come back and make it right — no questions asked, no awkwardness. Your satisfaction matters more than anything, and we mean that.

Do you clean homes with pets?

Absolutely. We're very used to working around pets and pet hair. Just let us know when you book so we can bring the right tools. We love animals — they usually love us back.

Can I add extra rooms or areas to my regular clean?

Of course. Just mention it when you book or reach out ahead of time and we'll adjust the time and price accordingly. We never want you to feel like the scope is rigid — it's your home.
