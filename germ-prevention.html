More Than Clean — Sanitized and Disinfected

Regular cleaning removes dirt and debris. Germ prevention goes further, targeting the pathogens that can make your family or coworkers sick.

A surface can look clean and still harbor bacteria and viruses that spread illness. Germ prevention service is designed to address exactly that — using professional-grade disinfectants on the surfaces and areas that matter most for reducing transmission.

We focus on the places where germs live and travel: doorknobs and handles, light switches, countertops, faucets, toilet surfaces, remote controls, keyboards — every high-touch surface in your space gets treated with the attention it needs.

This service is especially valuable during cold and flu season, after illness in the household, for clients with elderly family members at home, or anytime you want the extra assurance that your space is genuinely sanitized, not just clean-looking.

Germ Prevention Focuses On

  • All high-touch surfaces throughout the home or office
  • Door handles, knobs, and locks on every door
  • Light switches and outlet covers
  • Kitchen — counters, sink, faucet handles, appliance controls
  • Bathrooms — all surfaces, fixtures, and hardware
  • Railings, handles, and grab bars
  • Remote controls, keyboards, and shared electronics
  • Tables and chair armrests

Germ prevention can be scheduled on its own or added to any regular cleaning visit.

When Germ Prevention Is Right for You

There are several situations where professional disinfection makes a meaningful difference.

Cold & Flu Season

Keep your household healthier through the season by regularly treating high-touch surfaces. A single sick household member can be kept from spreading illness to the rest of the family.

After Illness in the Home

Once someone has been sick, a thorough disinfection breaks the chain of transmission before others in the household get sick too. Especially helpful for families with young children or seniors.

Homes With Elderly Residents

Older adults and those with compromised immune systems are more vulnerable to illness. Germ prevention adds a meaningful layer of protection for the people you love most.

Offices & Shared Spaces

Shared workspaces spread illness fast. We treat offices, waiting rooms, and other shared environments to reduce illness-related absenteeism and keep your team healthy.

Safe Products, Professional Results

We use professional-grade disinfectants that are effective against a broad spectrum of bacteria and viruses. If you or a family member has sensitivities, allergies, or specific product preferences, let us know when you schedule — we'll do our best to accommodate.

Every product we use is applied according to proper contact time requirements to ensure it's actually effective — not just sprayed on and wiped off immediately. That's the difference between a real disinfection and one that only looks like it was done.
