Full Service Area Coverage

Pro Cleaning Services is based in Brookville, Kansas and provides residential and commercial cleaning throughout central Kansas. Our primary service area includes all of Saline County, including the city of Salina — the largest city in central Kansas — along with the communities of Brookville, Assaria, Smolan, and Gypsum.

We regularly travel to McPherson County, serving McPherson, Marquette, Galva, Canton, and surrounding towns. We also serve Ottawa County, including Minneapolis and Tescott; Lincoln County, including the city of Lincoln and Sylvan Grove; Ellsworth County, including Ellsworth, Kanopolis, and Holyrood; and Dickinson County, including Abilene and Solomon.

If your community is not listed here, please give us a call at 785-452-0793. We will let you know right away whether we can accommodate your location. We are always looking to serve more of the central Kansas community.
